Welcome

Hello, I’m John Wallis. I provide computer support in Mildenhall, Newmarket, Bury St. Edmunds, and the surrounding areas of Suffolk. Although I do a small amount of work for local businesses, my speciality is solving domestic computer problems. For a reasonable charge I provide expert assistance with any of the following things:

  • troubleshooting (fixing misbehaving and non-working computers)
  • maintenance (cleaning, speeding up, and upgrading systems)
  • tuition (searching and buying online, using e-mail, and all other aspects of computing)
  • advice (choosing computers, software, and services)
  • the Internet (creating Web sites and e-mail accounts).

For speedy resolution of your computer-related problems, call or e-mail me . I’m efficient, reliable, happy to help, and avoid technical waffle. Although I understand computers inside out, I’ll never bore you with the details (unless you ask, and even then I’ll shut up whenever you like). Also, for a computer person, I’m unusually friendly and approachable. Almost normal really.

Take a look at the reviews page to see what some of my customers have said about me. They’re all five-star reviews! So if you have a broken or misbehaving computer or laptop in Mildenhall, Newmarket, Bury St. Edmunds, or anywhere else within a reasonable distance, please get in touch with me.

By the way, I only trade under my own name, John Geoffrey Wallis, not under multiple names like some other computer support people. I don’t play games in an attempt to get ranked higher by Google, and if you try to contact me then you’ll actually get me, not someone else.
