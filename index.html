High-quality computer support

Hello, I’m John Wallis. I provide high-quality computer support in Mildenhall, Newmarket, Bury St. Edmunds, Thetford, and the surrounding areas. Although I do some work for local businesses, my speciality is solving domestic computer problems.

As an experienced software developer, I am more than just a technician and can solve complex problems that require in-depth knowledge of computer systems and related devices, such as printers, routers, etc. So don’t hesitate to get in touch if you need help with any of the following:

  • troubleshooting (fixing misbehaving and non-working computers and laptops)
  • maintenance (cleaning, speeding up, and upgrading computers and laptops)
  • setup (setting up new systems, refreshing existing systems, and decommissioning old systems)
  • tuition (teaching people how to use computers and laptops in many different ways)
  • advice (choosing computers, software, and services)
  • the Internet (creating websites and e-mail accounts)
  • programming (building software to solve specific problems)
  • writing (creating and editing various document types).

For speedy resolution of your computer-related problems, call or e-mail me. I’m efficient, reliable, happy to help, and avoid technical waffle. I’ll never bore you with the details, unless you ask.
