What my customers think

Sources of reviews

Once I’ve finished a job for a customer, I often ask if they would be kind enough to review me on the Internet. I’m always very grateful to people who take the time to review me, because even though it doesn’t take very long, customers are often busy.

Of course some customers are nervous about making comments on the Internet, so my online reviews reflect only a small fraction of the work that I’ve done. Also some jobs are so quick and simple that it’s not reasonable to ask for a review.

As of 19th April 2017, I have a total of 39 online reviews (all five-star) spread across the following three independent sites:

FreeIndex

29 reviews (all five-star)

FreeIndex is a major source of reviews covering many different trades and professions.

Google

6 reviews (all five-star)

Google is probably the most popular and highly regarded search engine in the world.

Yell™

4 reviews (all five-star)

Yell™ is the online version of Yellow Pages™, a long-established index of goods and services.
