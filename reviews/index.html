What my customers think

Asking for reviews

Once I’ve finished a job for a customer, I often ask if they would be kind enough to write a review. I’m always very grateful to those who have taken the time to do this for me, because even though it doesn’t take very long, customers are often busy. Of course some customers dislike computers so much that they’re too nervous to post a review, so sometimes I do a good job, as usual, but nobody else will ever know. Unfortunately this goes with the territory.
