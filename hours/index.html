Above and beyond

If you have an emergency then I’ll often be able to attend outside normal working hours, including Sundays and Bank Holidays, with the exception of Christmas Day and Easter Sunday for legal reasons. By the way, my charges remain the same as for ordinary working days, so there’s no additional cost for out-of-hours working.
