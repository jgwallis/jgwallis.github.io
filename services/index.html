Services

Troubleshooting

Tracking down and solving problems is what I do most of the time, and having seen many of them before, I can tackle them quite quickly.

Maintenance

Making systems run efficiently is important, so why put up with sluggish performance when you can easily have your equipment sped up?

Setup

Unpacking new equipment and setting it all up can be quite daunting if you’ve never done it before, so why not let me do it for you?

Tuition

Although I have been a computer professional for years, I still remember what it was like to use a computer for the first time. So if you need some computer lessons, I am very patient and understanding.

Advice

If you are buying a new computer, why just pick one at random? Ask me what to get, and I’ll steer you away from the not-so-great models and explain all the different features in easy-to-understand terms.

Internet

The Internet is for everyone, not just technical people, so why not have a go and find out what it’s all about? Nothing scary is going to happen, and it’s actually useful in all kinds of ways, interesting, and fun.

Programming

Unlike most computer support people I have a background in programming, which is what I used to do full time for many years. So if you would like some software written to solve a specific problem, why not get in touch to see if it’s feasible? There’s never any harm in asking.

Writing

Like most programmers, I used to spend a lot of time writing documents about analysing, designing, programming, and testing systems. I have also written a lot of letters and drafted a lot of contracts over the years, so am experienced in writing plain, unambiguous English.
