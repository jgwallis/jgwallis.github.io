New site development

I have always been so busy fixing computer problems and creating Web sites for other people that I never got around to making one for myself. When I started providing domestic computer support I reasoned that if somebody had a computer problem, they might not be able to get onto the Internet to ask for help. Therefore it would make more sense to distribute flyers in the local area than to waste time creating a Web site. How wrong I was.

Most people now have access to more than one device for getting onto the Internet, so if one of these devices breaks they simply use another one to look for help online. Since I realised this, I have had more business from online searches than from all the flyers that I laboriously delivered by hand. However, rather than give up one medium in favour of another, I have decided to continue using both.
